Acne

Understanding Acne: Causes, Types, and Effective Treatments

What is Acne?

Acne is a common skin condition that affects people of all ages. It occurs when hair follicles become clogged with oil and dead skin cells, leading to blemishes. It can significantly impact self-esteem and quality of life.

Types of Acne

  • Comedonal Acne: Whiteheads and blackheads.
  • Inflammatory Acne: Papules and pustules.
  • Nodular Acne: Painful lumps beneath the skin.
  • Cystic Acne: Large, pus-filled cysts.

Causes of Acne

  • Hormonal Changes: Fluctuations during puberty or hormonal therapies.
  • Excess Oil Production: Overactive sebaceous glands clogging pores.
  • Bacteria: Propionibacterium acnes causing inflammation.
  • Diet: Diets high in sugar and dairy might worsen acne.
  • Stress: Can trigger hormonal changes and worsen acne.
  • Medications: Some medications contribute to acne development.
