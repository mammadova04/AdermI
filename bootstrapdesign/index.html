Our Story: The Birth of AdermI

AdermI was born from a shared vision to make skin health accessible for everyone. We realized how challenging it is to assess skin conditions without professional guidance, leading many to face uncertainty and delays in treatment. This resonated with our team as we saw friends and family struggle with skin issues, often unsure if their conditions were serious or easily treatable.The pivotal moment came when our AI/Computer Vision Specialist, Narmin Mammadova, explored AI's potential in healthcare. She realized that personal skin health was largely untapped. With her expertise in computer vision and machine learning, she saw an opportunity to create a tool for quick skin assessments using AI

Narmin Mammadova is our AI and Computer Vision Specialist. With extensive experience in AI and machine learning, she developed the models that recognize skin problems. Her expertise and passion for technology make her the perfect leader for developing our cutting-edge skin analysis solution.
Aysu Mammadova is the Front-End Developer responsible for creating an intuitive and user-friendly interface, ensuring the AdermI app is easy and pleasant to use.
Nahid Isayev is the Back-End Developer who manages the server-side logic, integrates the AI models, and handles data processing, ensuring the system runs smoothly.
Aziz Azizli is the Full-Stack Developer who bridges the front-end and back-end, ensuring the seamless performance and integration of the entire application.
